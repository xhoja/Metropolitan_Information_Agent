## Organization Overview

University Metropolitan Tirana (UMT) is a private higher education institution in Albania founded in 2010. It offers bachelor, master, and doctoral programmes across three faculties. UMT holds an **"Excellent"** accreditation rating from the British Quality Assurance Agency (QAA) for five consecutive years, alongside institutional accreditation from the Albanian Quality Assurance Agency in Higher Education. It is the **first university in Albania** to offer a PhD programme in Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems.

## Problem Statement — The Current Situation

### Pain Points

#### For Students
- **No self-service access to academic progress** — Students must request transcripts, GPA calculations, and graduation timelines from registrar office (24-48 hour turnaround)
- **Limited academic guidance** — Must schedule appointments with human advisers; waiting times average 5-7 days
- **Fragmented assignment workflow** — Assignment submission via email to professors (easy to lose track)
- **No unified academic dashboard** — Courses, grades, attendance scattered across different portals and email
- **Financial opacity** — No visibility into tuition balance, payment deadlines, or payment status
- **Poor course planning support** — No system to track which electives satisfy requirements or graduation timeline

#### For Professors
- **Multiple systems for course management** — Uses email, spreadsheets, Moodle, and paper records
- **Manual grade entry** — Each professor enters grades individually; no standardized format or validation
- **Attendance tracking is tedious** — Manual roll call or spreadsheet maintenance
- **Limited visibility into student progress** — Cannot easily identify at-risk students before final exams
- **Time-consuming roster management** — Manual enrollment adjustments require coordination with registrar
- **No organized assignment submission workflow** — Students submit via email; hard to track, grade, and provide feedback

#### For Administrators
- **User management is decentralized** — Student and professor accounts managed across multiple systems
- **No unified reporting** — Reports generated manually or from disconnected databases
- **Financial operations not integrated** — Tuition, payments, scholarships tracked separately from academic system
- **Audit trail is weak** — Difficult to track who changed what data and when
- **Enrollment management is manual** — Adding/removing students requires coordination across systems
- **Compliance risk** — Limited visibility into academic operations for accreditation and institutional reporting

## Requirements & Vision

### What Metropolitan University Wants

Metropolitan University seeks a **unified, modern, web-based platform** that consolidates all academic operations into a single source of truth. The platform should:

#### 1. **Eliminate System Fragmentation**
- Provide one unified interface for students, professors, and administrators
- Replace disconnected email, spreadsheets, and disparate portals
- Ensure data consistency across the institution

#### 2. **Enable Self-Service for Students**
- Access grades, GPA, attendance, and transcript in real time (no waiting for registrar)
- View and submit assignments with clear status tracking
- Get personalized academic guidance without waiting for adviser appointments
- See tuition balance, payment schedule, and transaction history

#### 3. **Streamline Professor Operations**
- Single dashboard for courses, grades, attendance, assignments, and materials
- Bulk operations for efficiency (upload grades for entire class at once)
- Clear visibility into student roster and class progress
- Unified feedback mechanism on assignments

#### 4. **Give Administrators Complete Control**
- User management with role-based access control
- Full visibility into academic operations and data
- Financial management (fees, payments, scholarships)
- Reporting and analytics for institutional planning
- Audit trail for compliance and policy enforcement

#### 5. **Add Intelligence with AI**
- **Conversational AI academic adviser** that helps students without human involvement
- Students ask questions naturally: "When do I graduate?", "What electives should I take?"
- System answers based on student's progress, major, and graduation requirements
- Remembers student preferences and learning history across conversations

#### 6. **Manage Financial Operations**
- Configure tuition fees per major/program
- Auto-assign fee records and installment plans to students
- Track payments online
- Generate financial reports for planning and compliance

## Scope & Constraints

### Scope

#### In Scope (MVP)
- 3 role-based dashboards (Admin, Professor, Student)
- Course and enrollment management
- Grade recording and attendance tracking
- Assignment submission and feedback
- M.I.A AI academic adviser (student portal only)
- User authentication and role-based access control
- Basic reporting (enrollment, grades)

#### Out of Scope (Phase 2)
- Scholarship management system
- Advanced financial analytics
- Mobile app (Phase 2)
- Integration with external systems (alumni portal, third-party LMS)
- Document processing (OCR, handwriting recognition)
- Video conferencing integration

### Constraints

| Constraint | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Timeline** | Must launch by end of academic year (June 2026) for fall semester rollout |
| **Budget** | Fixed budget; cannot expand significantly; emphasize value over bells & whistles |
| **Data Privacy** | Must comply with Albanian data protection laws; student data is sensitive |
| **Language** | Support Albanian as primary language (English as secondary) |
| **Scalability** | Must support full student body + 30% projected growth over 3 years |
| **Integration** | Must not disrupt existing systems during transition |
| **Training** | Limited IT literacy among some faculty; system must be intuitive |
